How to Score

Group stage (running total):

  1. After each day's games, click Refresh data
  2. Click the winner of each new match
  3. Click Score New Results
  4. Click Save All Scores to Sheet

Knockout rounds:

  1. After round ends, click Refresh data
  2. Enter team names + click the winner
  3. Click Score All Participants
  4. Click Save All Scores to Sheet
